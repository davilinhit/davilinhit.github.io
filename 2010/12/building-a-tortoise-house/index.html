Building a tortoise pen

December 2010 - Portland, Oregon

I inherited Tucker the Tortoise from a bored nephew and felt it was unfair to subject him to a life of fear with only a small aquarium’s glass between him and two very interested cats. I gave him to my dad and we built a 4′×8′ wood pen for him at the bottom of the garden.

Update: Three years later, he’s since moved on to someone else’s house.

The moral of the story is that turtles are not toys and you shouldn’t buy them for your kids.
