Zen

“Enlightenment is a lofty word, but its reality is something quite ordinary. Th enlightened person is simply the person who isn’t selfish, who sees things as they really are, loves them, and acts out of that love. With zazen practice we see a world that is lovely, and that calls out to us to participate in it. We are glad to do it. We can’t not do it.” (pg xxix)
