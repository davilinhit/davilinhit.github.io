About
Hi! I'm an electrical engineer and open hardware hacker living near Portland, Oregon. Lately I've been reading, hiking, growing vegetables, learning to sea kayak, and writing informal, photo-heavy posts for far-flung friends and family.

I designed the hardware and software for aircraft cameras with Astronics Max-Viz, and now I help provide purple printed circuit boards at OSHPark. I release all my code and schematics as Wicker on Github.

I'm available through Wickerbox Electronics to design circuits, lay out boards, write low-level microcontroller code, develop documentation, give a talk, and/or offer project advice. Feel free to email me at jeh.wicker@gmail.com.
