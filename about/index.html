Hi, I'm Jenner Hanni.

I'm an aerospace and embedded systems geek from Portland, Oregon.

I'll finish my M.Eng in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Portland State this winter. I'm a generalist so my open source projects include hardware, software, networking, and websites. I'm currently designing a drone made of composites. I helped build and repair homebuilt aircraft as part of Experimental Aircraft Association Chapter 590 while serving five years as a U.S. Marine working on jet and helicopter avionics in Yuma, Arizona.

In my spare time I'm a private pilot and world traveler. I've backpacked in Europe from London to Sweden to Turkey, drove and traveled by train across the United States and Canada, and deployed both in Iraq and aboard the USS Tarawa to the Persian Gulf via Singapore and Australia.
