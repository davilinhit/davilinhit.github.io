Hi, I'm Jenner.

I'm an electrical engineer and open hardware hacker living near Portland, Oregon. You can find my work on GitHub and I'll be giving a talk at Open Source Bridge 2015 called Open Source your Circuit Design with KiCAD.

I designed the hardware and software for the MV-1200 and MV-1400 aircraft cameras with Astronics Max-Viz, and now I help provide purple printed circuit boards at OSHPark.

I'm available through Wickerbox Electronics to design circuits, lay out boards, write low-level microcontroller code, develop documentation, give a talk, and/or offer project advice. Feel free to email me at jeh.wicker@gmail.com.
