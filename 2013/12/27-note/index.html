Friday, December 27 2013

Spent a couple days in a cabin at Kalaloch Lodge on the Olympic Peninsula. I edited some posts and got everything ready for when I get home to just push the site.

I have a couple interesting projects in the pipeline, including the RF board and an implementation of Timo Bergmann’s Sound of Sorting. I never had the algorithms class at school so writing fifteen sorts with the help of the Intro to Algorithms, 3rd Ed, has introduced me to a bunch of concepts – optimization among them.
