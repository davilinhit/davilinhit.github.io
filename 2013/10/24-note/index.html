Building the 38mm Sentinel Rocket »

Thursday, October 24 2013

Still foggy.

I’m still having some forearm pain so I skipped BJJ and came home to figure out how to break the reading-of-articles that I want to do away from my compulsive browsing-of-Reddit. I have a wifi-only, first-gen iPad for reading and a first-gen (I think) Kindle for reading on backpacking/hiking trips. I added Instapaper to my Firefox on all my computers (work, home desktop, laptop) and dropped all pending bookmarks in there.

I’m using Trello for a general tracking-of-everything and I don’t want this to be a part of that at all. I also can email PDFs to myself to get them into the Kindle App. That should be enough to make this stuff available to me. And I can save links from Ars or Reddit or whatever for later, without wasting cycles worrying that I’ll never see it again.

Hopefully this’ll help clear my head. I have a pile of autonomous quadcopter papers I want to read.

I posted the photos from building my 38mm Sentinel rocket from Binder Designs.

I made lemon poppyseed scones that were actually pretty amazing. Also have discovered lemon curd.
