Saturday, October 26 2013

Still foggy but the afternoons have been clear blue sky.

I drove in to downtown to hack with greh at Portland State. Started out by putting Raspbian on my Raspberry Pi. I’ve exhausted Wicker Man references for my servers so on greh’s advice, I’m calling it rasputin. At least this way, I should be unable to kill it.

I picked up the IMEX Firefox that showed up in the lab and ripped the guts out, replacing the IMEX control board with an Arduino and Adafruit motor shield. The motor clicks but I swapped servos as well and that works great.

My cat has decided that waking at 3am and show me affection by licking my head until I get out of bed is an A++ way to contribute to a happy and functioning household. I’m not sure how to convince my cat that it’s pretty fucking wrong.
