Friday, November 22 2013

Found Sumana‘s blog this week and I keep returning to it. I saw her keynote at Open Source Bridge and now she’s taking a sabbatical from her post at Wikimedia to do Hacker School, which is cool to read about. I ended up reading her back entries and her blog’s kind of the sort of thing I’d like for mine — technical stuff mixed with “hey, this is what I did today” with some observations.

The thing is, I think I’m afraid of writing the wrong thing and so I don’t write at all, but it’s like there’s this idea of what I’ll write and how I’ll feel writing it that when I don’t achieve that headspace then I’m like “oops, can’t write today, oh well” and blow it off. I’d like to stop doing that. Just write already.
