Sunday, November 3 2013

Drove over to Cape Lookout on the coast for the morning. Low fog in the valleys and it’s finally tilted over into cold. Turned the heat on when I realized the cats were cuddling a little more desperately than normal.

I’ve focused on work and BJJ this week but I bought the Pololu VNH5019 motor driver shield which can handle over 10 A for a single motor. I figured out the wiring I’ll need for the Foxcar once that gets here. I’m probably going to do a custom board with:

- an STM32F4 processor

- XBee wireless (need to explore RF transceivers – I know nothing about RC hobby RF stuff, except that there’s a ton of it out there and has been forever.

- camera that’s fast enough for real-time analysis, so I had to discard a couple I found through Adafruit and Sparkfun.

- 9DOF Sparkfun sensor board

- micro SD card for data logging or image logging

- micro-USB and JTAG

Actually, putting a Node 6 from PSAS on it might be a good idea.
