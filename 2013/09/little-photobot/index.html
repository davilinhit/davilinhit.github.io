Little Photobot

September 2013 - Newberg, Oregon

Two photoresistors are wired as shown below so this bot can be either photophobic or photophilic based on how the motors are installed. This bot will be photophobic, turning away from the light source, if the wired motor is installed on the same side as its photoresistor. If photoresistor and motor are on opposite sides, the bot will appear photophilic, turning towards the light source.
