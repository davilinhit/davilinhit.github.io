Foxcar Drive and Turn Demo

November 2013 - Portland, Oregon

I bought a Pololu VNH5019 Dual Motor Driver Carrier Shield for Arduino and wired in the DC motor. With stacking headers I could use pin 2 for the servo and control both from the Arduino. I’m using mem’s ServoTimer2 and Pololu’s VNH5019 Motor Driver Shield libraries.

The code’s in the Github repo under ‘hardware-test’.

This is the hardware test. You can see in the video that I’m driving a servo to turn the front wheels and a heavy-duty motor to roll the wheels forward and backward.

Next step: custom control board.
