Saturday, February 1 2014

I was a little bummed this morning that January’s over and I don’t have any other hardware stuff to post about but I realized that I’ve been super busy doing, you know, work for which I’m paid and can’t talk about. On the hobby hacking side, I’ve done plenty of work this month between the oven controller, a couple of GPS RF board design reviews, the DxWiFi test, and my BJJ habit. I’ve also had four sessions of EPL managing where other people came in to work on stuff.

One of my biggest reasons for having the site is to remind myself that I do things by posting pictures of the stuff I do. I tend to forget and then blame myself for being Not Productive Enough.

So looking back, I’m delighted to remember that I had a really enjoyable January.
