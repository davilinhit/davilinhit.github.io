Building Raised Beds

Getting started with the garden this year. Last year I did all right with veggies but they were in the ground and it could have been better. Also, I have the smallest backyard ever.

There are three 3′×6′ beds about 20" high. I used cedar fence boards and pine studs, then coated them with linseed oil. Total cost was about $100 and one day’s work.
